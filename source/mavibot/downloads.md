## Maven Dependency

The Apache Mavibot package is also available as a Maven dependency:

    <dependency>
        <groupId>org.apache.directory.mavibot</groupId>
        <artifactId>mavibot</artifactId>
        <version>${mavibot-version}</version>
    </dependency>
